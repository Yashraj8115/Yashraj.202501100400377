Attractions & Activities

Jaipur


Visit the Amber Fort and City Palace.
Shop for handicrafts and gems in the local bazaars.
Do not miss the Hawa Mahal, a symbol of Jaipur’s beauty.

Goa


Relax on the Baga and Anjuna beaches.
Enjoy water sports like parasailing and jet-skiing.
Experience Goa’s silent nights vibrant nightlife!

Leh-Ladakh


Ride through the Khardung La Pass, one of the highest roads in the world.
Camp near Pangong Lake.
Visit monasteries with colorful prayer flags fluttering in the wind.

Kerala


Enjoy a peaceful houseboat cruise in Alleppey.
Witness Kathakali dance performances.
Rejuvenate with Ayurvedic spa therapies.

Varanasi


Attend the sacred Ganga Aarti on the ghats.
Explore narrow alleys filled with temples.
Take a boat ride at sunrise for a magical experience.
